Sabrina Simms has over 10 years of experience supporting several government initiatives in the areas of Agile project management, requirements development, project management, and change management


Sabrina is currently supporting the USPTO Office of Finance in retiring their legacy financial system and the development of a new Fee Processing Next Generation System for fee collection and refunds processing.
